
# Vocabulary: Engineering excellence

- **KISS:** smallest structure that solves today's problem
- **YAGNI:** no speculative layers, factories, or "future-proof" generics
- **DRY:** one source of truth: catalog in `reuse-catalog.md`
- **SOLID:** thin routes/views; business logic in services/composables
- **Testable:** smoke specs per view; pure logic unit-tested
- **Living docs:** `.heyeddi/docs/engineering/` updated when structure changes
- **ADR:** Architecture Decision Record in `decisions.md`: not design tokens
